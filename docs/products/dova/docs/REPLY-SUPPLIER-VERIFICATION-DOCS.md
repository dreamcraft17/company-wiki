**Re:** “Which type of document for verification?”

Thanks for the question. The supplier form accepts **PDF, JPG, or PNG (max 5 MB)**. For **what to upload**, we use the following for MVP:

## Accepted documents (upload **one**)

1. **CAC / Business Name Registration** — preferred for registered companies  
2. **Valid government ID** of the owner/contact — NIN slip, National ID, Driver’s Licence, or International Passport  
3. Optional for farms: **proof of farm/business address** (e.g. utility bill)

Admin reviews the upload before the supplier account is approved.

## Product update

This guidance is now shown **on the supplier registration form** (`/auth/supplier-register`) so applicants are not confused.
