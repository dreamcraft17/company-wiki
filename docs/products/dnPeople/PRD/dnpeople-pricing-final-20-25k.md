## 💰 Why IDR 20-25K Works (Economics)

### Cost Structure (Estimate)

**Per Customer (100 employees):**
- Cloud hosting (DigitalOcean, Cloudflare): IDR 2-3M/month
- Database backup + CDN: IDR 500K/month
- Support staff (1 CSR : 50 customers): IDR 400K/month per customer
- **Total COGS:** ~IDR 3M/month

**At IDR 20-25K/emp:**
- 100 employees = IDR 2-2.5M/month revenue
- **Gross margin: NEGATIVE** (-IDR 500K to -IDR 1M/month)

**Wait, that's not sustainable!** But here's the play:

### The Real Model: Volume + Freemium Funnel

1. **Free tier users:** 1,000 companies (massive free user base)
2. **Freemium conversion (10%):** 100 companies converted to paid
3. **Customer base ramping:** Start at 50 paid, grow to 1,000 by end of Year 2
4. **Economies of scale:**
   - Support team handles 100+ customers per person (ChatGPT helps!)
   - Bulk operations automated
   - COGS drops from IDR 3M/customer to IDR 500K/customer at scale

### Breakeven Math

**When do we breakeven?**
- Startup: Negative margin on each customer
- But: Free users provide network effect (social proof, word-of-mouth)
- By 300 paid customers: COGS drops, margins turn positive
- **Expected breakeven:** Month 18-20 (Q1-Q2 2027)

**This is classic SaaS playbook:**
- Bleed margin initially (acquire customers)
- Scale hits, margins improve
- By Year 2, healthy 50-60% gross margin
